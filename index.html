Join Us

You know those two guys sitting at the bar talking sports, movies, music, tv shows and everything else under the sun and they really don't have a clue what they are talking about but you wish you were a part of the conversion? Well that's us! So grab a your favorite beverage, pull up a bar stool, crank up your stereo, put your headphones or whatever you do to listen to a podcast and relax and laugh along with us.
